## Blood Pressure Basics

Blood pressure measures the force of blood against artery walls.

## The Numbers

Systolic (top number) and diastolic (bottom number) readings indicate heart function.

## Healthy Ranges

Understanding what constitutes healthy blood pressure helps prevent complications.

## Management

Lifestyle changes and medication can help maintain optimal levels.
